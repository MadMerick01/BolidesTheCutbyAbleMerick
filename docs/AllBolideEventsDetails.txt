Bolide Events

NOTE: Do not alter this document. Treat it as the source of truth and align all event implementations to the details listed below.

Common fields to track for every event (include in each event entry):
-Trigger/activation conditions (distance, timing, prerequisites, cooldown)
-Spawn details (vehicle, config, spawn method, spawn position)
-Phase transitions (what moves Start -> Middle -> End, with thresholds)
-HUD status/instruction text (exact strings)
-Pause/mission message text (exact strings, active or not)
-Audio cues or effects
-Rewards/penalties (money delta, inventory items, charges/ammo)
-End outcomes and cleanup behavior (despawn, next-event chaining)

1. RobberEMP
	Start
	-what = the robber vehicle spawns and starts to move in on the player
	-Status message = A vehicle is tailing you
	-Pause message (not active)
	-colour = yellow

	Middle
	-what = the robber comes into range and deploys the EMP (and takes some of the players money)
	-status message = You've been robbed, chase the robber down and stop their vehicle to get it back
	-Pause message (not active)
	-colour = red

	End1
	-what = the player escapes to range before the robber can rob
	-status message = you live to fight another day
	-Pause message (active) message = you live to fight another day
	-colour = green

	End2
	-what = the player catches the robber and gets their money back and possible extra money or device and/or ammo
	-status message = You got your money back (and found x and/or y and/or z) (x = money, y = EMP or Pistol, z = charges or ammo)  
	-Pause message (active) message = You got your money back (and found x and/or y and/or z) (x = money, y = EMP or Pistol, z = charges or ammo)
	-colour = green

	End3
	-what - the robber escapes to range
	-status message = The robber escaped with your money, you lost x (where x is dollars lost)
	-Pause message (active) message = The robber escaped with your money, you lost x (where x is dollars lost)
	-colour = green
